Goal

Our goal is to provide the highest quality orthodontics for a fair price, in a professional yet casual atmosphere. Orthodontic treatment is a gift that will last a lifetime. It's an investment in self-confidence as well as good health and well-being. Having a beautiful smile will be appreciated by every person you encounter for the rest of yoru life! We normally do not charge for the initial exam. Dr. Joseph will make a quick examination to determine the problem.

If braces are indicated, we will attempt to answer the most important questions about treatment, such as:

  • How much will it cost?
  • How long will the braces be worn?
  • Will it be necessary for any teeth to be removed or for any srugical procedures?
