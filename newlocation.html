New Location!

New Year, New Office, New Smile!

After our old office was completely destroyed in the Joplin tornado, we relocated to a temporary office. On the 5 year anniversary of the tornado we moved to our new permanent location, across the street from our original office.

We are now located in the newly built Neighborhood Center, just west of the new Wal-Mart Neighborhood Market on 26th & Maiden Lane.
